1. Preparation and Planning

After you chose the right real estate agent, we will assess your finances to determine your budget and get pre-approved for a mortgage. Then, we will define your preferences and needs together to assist you with property searches and negotiations.

An experienced real estate agent can save you time and money by helping you find the right home and negotiating with the seller on your behalf. Agents are licensed professionals who know their markets well and can guide you through your home buying journey.

See our mortgage offer
2. Home search and Offer

With your budget in mind, we can begin searching for homes that meet your criteria, such as location, size, amenities and price range and start showing you properties.

Once we find you a home you're interested in, we will write up an offer to the seller. They may accept, decline or try to negotiate with you.

Meet our agent
3. Verification and Examination

During the examination phase, we will conduct inspections to ensure the home is in satisfactory condition and worth the purchase price. Simultaneously, youll finalize your mortgage with your lender.

This phase allows you to thoroughly assess the property and address any concerns before closing.
4. Closing

Once all contingencies are met, and financing is in place, we will proceed to the closing phase. At closing, you'll sign all the necessary paperwork to transfer ownership from the seller to you.

This includes mortgage documents, the deed, and various other legal documents, You'll also pay any remaining closing costs, such as closing costs, lender fees, property taxes and homeowner insurance.

Once everything is signed and funds are disbursed, youll receive the keys to your new home and the sale is complete.

You are officially a homeowner now!
